ABOUT

Dining Options

We take pride in our menu. Originally a fast food experience, our food preparation is optimized to bring your food quickly without compromising taste. Our newly revamped menu, containing more conventional and filling dishes, brings even more options to your table.

Reservations

Celebrating a birthday or the completion of a group project? Reserve a table (or multiple!) and enjoy a comfortable environment in a convenient on-campus location. We also offer personalized amenities for birthdays, graduation celebrations, and many holidays.

Relaxing Interior

Perfect for studying or just plain relaxing, our interior provides a comfortable environment to assist in whatever you need. Order some finger food and a drink to give you the energy to ace that practice test or nail that essay. Just take a table, alone or as a group, and enjoy your stay!
